Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti is a simple and quick dish that is perfect for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat marinara sauce in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Drain spaghetti and add to the sauce. Toss to coat.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Serve hot.
