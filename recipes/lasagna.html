Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and diced tomatoes. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
  5. Layer with lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
