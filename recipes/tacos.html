Tacos

Description

These tacos are a flavorful and fun dish that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Salsa, for serving

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water according to package instructions. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm taco shells according to package instructions.
  4. Fill taco shells with beef mixture. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and salsa.
